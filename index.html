Expierence

Unreal Engine

I have developed a variety of characters, pawns, and actors in Unreal Engine, ensuring they are fully functional and integrated into the game environment. My work with the Gameplay Ability System includes designing and implementing complex abilities, effects, and events. I've created animation blendspaces, state machines, and animation triggers to bring game assets to life. Additionally, I have experience creating behavior trees and custom nodes using C++ to implement advanced AI behaviors. I have also designed and implemented UI widgets to create intuitive and interactive user interfaces. Proficient in both Blueprints and C++, I create flexible and efficient game logic, using Blueprints for rapid prototyping and C++ for performance-critical functionalities.

Unity

I have extensive experience working with Unity, where I have developed a first-person shooter (FPS) game. My work includes creating complex boss characters with multiple mechanics, utilizing NavMesh for advanced pathfinding and AI navigation. I have implemented detailed animations and behavior trees to bring characters to life, along with animation triggers to ensure smooth transitions. Additionally, I have designed immersive environments and developed intuitive UI interfaces to enhance the player experience. My skills in Unity encompass a wide range of game development aspects, from AI behavior and animation to environment design and user interface creation, ensuring a comprehensive and engaging gameplay experience.

OpenGL

Invasion

Invasion is a first-person shooter developed in Unity, where players find themselves waking up after a crash landing on Mars. Drawing inspiration from iconic titles like Doom and Halo from the Xbox 360 era, the game blends classic FPS elements with modern design. Created as a midterm project at Full Sail University, Invasion was a collaborative effort where I played a crucial role in nearly every facet of development, including user interface design, artificial intelligence, combat mechanics, and level creation.

Dungeon Divers

Dungeon Divers is a modern homage to the classic arcade game, Venture. Players control a character navigating through a maze teeming with monsters. The objective is to collect all the treasure and successfully traverse each level to escape. In this project, I served as the graphics programmer. My contributions included implementing post-processing effects, Lambertian shading, specular reflection, and ambient lighting. Additionally, I designed the entire UI framework using Blender and custom C++ UI classes.
